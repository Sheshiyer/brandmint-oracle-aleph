# Viral Video Hooks

## 1. The "Negative" Hook
**Audio/Text Overlay:**
"[Stop buying X until you see this...]"

**Visual Action:**
[Person aggressively throwing competitor product in bin / or shaking head]

**First 15s Script:**
(0:00-0:03) Stop buying [Competitor Category] until you see this.
(0:03-0:08) Most of them break in a week, but we just fixed the design.
(0:08-0:15) Watch this... [Transition to demo]

## 2. The "Curiosity" Hook
**Audio/Text Overlay:**
"[I bet you didn't know this existed...]"

**Visual Action:**
[Extreme close-up of the product's unique feature]

**First 15s Script:**
(0:00-0:04) I bet you didn't know this existed.
(0:04-0:09) It looks like a normal [Object], but press this button...
(0:09-0:15) [Action happens] Boom. Game changer.

## 3. The "Problem/Solution" Hook
**Audio/Text Overlay:**
"[POV: You finally fixed [Pain Point]]"

**Visual Action:**
[Satisfying video of the problem disappearing]

**First 15s Script:**
(0:00-0:05) POV: You finally fixed [Pain Point] without [Annoying Thing].
(0:05-0:10) I used to hate [Task], but this little tool...
(0:10-0:15) It does the work for you. Link in bio.

# Illustration Style Prompt Templates

## POSTERS

### Template: Grid Poster Design (Amir Mushich 4.7)
**Use for:** Campaign visual identity grid with halftone photography

[BRAND NAME]. Act as a graphic design creative director constructing a "Campaign Visual Identity Grid."

COMPOSITION (2-COLUMN ASYMMETRICAL LAYOUT):
ROW 1: One single, full-width rectangular block (wide photo).
ROW 2: Two equal-sized square blocks side-by-side.
ROW 3: One single, full-width rectangular block.
ROW 4: Two equal-sized square blocks side-by-side.
ROW 5: One single, full-width rectangular block at the bottom.

CRITICAL CONSTRAINTS:
- FULL BLEED (100% COVERAGE): NO margins, NO borders, NO background space visible.
- ZERO SPACING (NO GAPS): Tiles seamlessly abutted. No gutters or white lines.

THE GRID CONTENT (3 TYPES):
TYPE A: THE LOGO BLOCK - Solid brand primary color, centered logomark, distressed screen-print texture.
TYPE B: THE SLOGAN BLOCK - Contrasting brand color, official slogan, heavy distressed ink texture.
TYPE C: CAMPAIGN PHOTOGRAPHY - High-contrast B&W action shots with strong halftone dot patterns (raster dots) and heavy film grain. "Multiply" color blending in brand palette (duotone effect).

Typography: ONLY ONE font. Every photo cell unique. No duplicates.

### Template: Christmas Branded Gifts Tree (Amir Mushich 4.9)
**Use for:** Holiday-themed brand illustration

A charming, hand-drawn fashion illustration of a Christmas tree constructed entirely from a chaotic stack of [BRAND NAME] product boxes.

COMPOSITION: Boxes piled in pyramid shape mimicking Christmas tree silhouette. Golden star at top.
BRAND PALETTE: Boxes colored strictly in [BRAND NAME]'s official color palette. Visible logos, shipping labels, barcodes, tape.
ART STYLE: "Alcohol Marker and Ink" sketch on white paper. Loose, artistic, vibrant.

BACKGROUND: Clean white paper texture.

## ICONS

### Template: Notion-styled Character Icon (Amir Mushich 5.1)
**Use for:** Black-and-white vector character portraits

A minimalist, black-and-white flat vector illustration portrait of [CHARACTER NAME], rendered in the expressive "Notion avatar" art style.

LINE WORK: THICK, UNIFORM black outlines (monoline weight). Organic, smooth, confident lines.
SELECTIVE BLACK FILLS: Solid black for hair masses, beards, eyebrows, sunglasses, dark clothing. Skin and face remain pure flat white.
COMPOSITION: No shading, no gray tones, no gradients. Only pure Black and pure White. Clean bust or headshot on white background.

### Template: Custom Branded Sticker Pack (Amir Mushich 5.3)
**Use for:** 8-10 piece branded sticker sheet

[BRAND NAME]. Act as a vector illustrator designing a trendy "Sticker Sheet" set.

TASK: Deconstruct brand identity into 8-10 unique sticker designs. Autonomously invent:
- Mascots/Characters: Stylized busts wearing brand accessories
- Key Products: Iconic items associated with the brand
- Symbols/Objects: Abstract shapes relevant to brand lifestyle

VISUAL STYLE (MONOLINE VECTOR):
Line Work: THICK, UNIFORM monoline weight. Smooth, rounded, confident.
Coloring: Solid Off-White or Cream fill. NO gradients, NO shading, NO 3D effects.
"Sticker" Effect: Thick White Die-Cut Border surrounding each element.

COLOR PALETTE: Deep saturated background color. High-brightness Cream/White stickers with Dark outlines.
COMPOSITION: Knolling layout, even spacing, no overlapping.

### Template: Minimalist Brand Monogram (Amir Mushich 5.4)
**Use for:** Ornamental single-letter monogram with brand logo

A flat, minimalist vector design featuring a highly stylized single letter monogram with the official brand logo below it, representing [BRAND NAME].

MAIN ELEMENT: ONLY the single first letter of [BRAND NAME]. "Ornamental Vector Typography" with high calligraphic artistry. Elegant flourishes, exaggerated swashes, ornamental serifs. Clean, precise vector graphics.
SECONDARY ELEMENT: Original official logomark below, rendered significantly smaller.
FINISH: Zero texture, zero embossing, zero shadows, zero gradients. Perfectly flat digital illustration.
COLOR: AI-driven high-contrast palette. Solid flat background + contrasting monogram + monochrome white or dark gray logo.

## TYPOGRAPHY ART

### Template: American Retro-lettering (Amir Mushich 8.2)
**Use for:** 1950s-70s baseball script / bubble lettering

A bold, vibrant, retro-American vector lettering design displaying the name "[TEXT]".

TYPOGRAPHY: Thick, chunky "baseball script" or "bubble" style. Heavy, rounded, tightly connected letters. Slightly slanted/arched. Thick bold outline + deep solid "block shadow" (extrusion).
COLOR: Bright, saturated, flat colors from brand identity filtered through retro pop-art lens. High contrast between fill, outline (white/cream), and block shadow.
DECORATIVE: Subtle Americana elements (swoosh underline, star, simplified icon). Don't over-detail.
BACKGROUND: Clean, pure white. No paper textures or noise.

### Template: Retro-typographic Heritage Rebranding (Amir Mushich 8.5)
**Use for:** 19th century copperplate engraving style logo

[BRAND NAME]. Act as a master engraver creating a minimalist "19th Century Heritage" logomark.

STYLE: "Copperplate Engraving" or "Scientific Botanical Illustration" from the 1800s.
Line Quality: Incredibly thin, delicate hairlines. Subtle cross-hatching only. Light, airy, precise.
No Heavy Borders: NO shields, crests, or thick frames. Open and airy composition.
THE SYMBOL: Reinterpret brand logo as vintage hand-drawn illustration. Like an illustration from an expensive 19th-century encyclopedia.
TYPOGRAPHY: High-Contrast Roman Serif (Didone style). Wide, elegant letter spacing.
MATERIAL: Clean solid Off-White or Cream paper. Black or Deep Charcoal ink.

### Template: Swiss Design Typographic Poster (Amir Mushich 8.6)
**Use for:** Minimalist poster with frosted glass overlay

A minimalist Swiss Design poster for the work entitled "[TITLE]".

Design: Ascetic, legible, bold sans-serif typography. Clean neutral background with strict grid-based composition.
GLASS ELEMENT: Large, frosted, semi-translucent glass overlay partially covering the typography. Creates subtle distortion, refraction, or blurring on text beneath. Adds depth and tactile quality.
COLOR: Monochrome or strictly limited muted, sophisticated tones.
AESTHETIC: Clean, intellectual, highly organized. Soft studio lighting emphasizing glass texture and paper.

## Style Selection Guide

| Need | Use Template | Why |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Brand campaign collateral | Grid Poster | Full-bleed halftone campaign boards |
| Social media avatar | Notion Icon | Clean B&W, works at any size |
| Brand merch stickers | Sticker Pack | Die-cut ready, monoline vector |
| Artistic exploration | Woodblock Print | Unique analog aesthetic |
| Character for series | Anime Character | Personality-driven, consistent style |
| Logo treatment | Embossed or Heritage | 3D surface or vintage engraving |
| Event poster | Swiss Design | Intellectual, frosted glass effect |
| Fun brand typography | Retro Lettering | Nostalgic, thumb-stopping |

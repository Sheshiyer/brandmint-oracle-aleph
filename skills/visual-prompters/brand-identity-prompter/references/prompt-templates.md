## Section 2: Brand Identity & Logo Styles

### 2.1 Brand Kit from One Prompt

```
[BRAND NAME] ({INDUSTRY}).
Act as Lead Brand Designer creating a comprehensive "Brand Identity System" presentation (Bento-Grid Layout).

Generate a single high-resolution bento-grid board containing 6 distinct modules:

PHASE 1: VISUAL STRATEGY (AUTONOMOUS)
1. Analyze the Brand: Determine archetype and visual vibe
2. Define the Palette: Select 4 distinct colors
3. Select Typography: Choose ONE specific Google Font

PHASE 2: THE LAYOUT (6-MODULE GRID)
Block 1 (The Hero): High-end Key Visual photography. Cinematic lighting, photorealistic. Overlay brand logo in white.
Block 2 (Social Media): Instagram Post mockup with bold headline and lifestyle image.
Block 3 (The Palette): 4 Vertical Color Swatches with simulated HEX Codes inside.
Block 4 (Typography Spec): ONLY font name displayed in that font. NO alphabet (Aa Bb Cc). Tiny "Primary Typeface" subtext.
Block 5 (The Logo): Final Logo on solid background. Clean vector, NO construction lines.
Block 6 (Brand DNA): Manifesto Card -- ARCHETYPE, VOICE, VISUALS paragraphs.

PHASE 3: AESTHETIC & FINISH
Style: Behance Trend / Awwwards Winner. Quality: 8k resolution. Soft studio lighting.
```

**Variables:** `[BRAND NAME]`, `{INDUSTRY}`
**Best for:** Complete brand identity presentation in a single image
**Output:** 6-module bento-grid board at 8K resolution
**Note:** Most complex template -- AI autonomously determines palette, typography, and archetype

### 2.2 Swiss Design Logos

```
A clean, minimalist retro-modernist poster featuring a simplified geometric abstraction of the [BRAND] logo.
Design must be reductive and uncluttered, using fewest possible geometric shapes (circles, squares, thick lines). Balanced iconic composition with generous negative space.
Color palette strictly limited to official brand colors (plus black or white), set against plain matte cream/off-white background. Flat vector style, sharp edges, no textures, no gradients. Swiss Design typography at the bottom.
```

**Variables:** `[BRAND]`
**Best for:** Retro-modernist logo reinterpretations, poster designs
**Style:** Swiss International Typographic Style, flat vector, no gradients

---

### 2.3 Glass Logos

```
3D embossed glossy contour render of center-aligned [BRAND] on a flat surface, perfectly centered with ample negative space. Monochromatic [COLOR] palette with soft tonal gradients. Raised smooth liquid-like glass bezel or chrome rim creating blind emboss effect where interior matches background. Matte surface finish with fine film grain or noise texture overlay. Soft diffuse lighting, strong specular highlights on rounded edges, top-down view.
```

**Variables:** `[BRAND]`, `[COLOR]`
**Best for:** Glass embossed logo renders, premium digital identity
**Style:** Monochromatic palette, blind emboss effect, film grain overlay

## Section 3: 3D Logo Treatments

### 3.1 Dark Metallic Logos

```
[BRAND NAME], the official trademark logo rendered strictly as a solid 3D object, maintaining exact original vector shape and proportions without artistic reinterpretation. Dark highly polished chrome metal with subtle surface micro-scratches breaking mirror finish. Perfectly centered in vast infinite pure white void. Soft cinematic bloom (halo effect) along metallic edges. 50mm lens, shallow DOF blurring furthest points. Pronounced visible film grain overlay.
```

**Variables:** `[BRAND NAME]`
**Best for:** Premium 3D logo renders, chrome metallic treatment
**Camera:** 50mm lens, shallow DOF
**Style:** Dark polished chrome, micro-scratches, cinematic bloom, film grain

---

### 3.2 Tactile Wax Seal Logos

```
[MATERIAL COLOR] glossy wax seal icon depicting [BRAND] logo, lying flat on plain white background. Thick deformed plastic or wax with irregular squashed shape and prominent drip/smear extending to right side. Clear raised relief impression of subject in center. Soft even lighting, smooth specular highlights on curved glossy edges. Soft minimal shadow.
```

**Variables:** `[MATERIAL COLOR]`, `[BRAND]`
**Best for:** Tactile logo treatments, wax seal brand marks
**Style:** Glossy deformed wax/plastic, raised relief, soft lighting

---

### 3.3 Logo Retexturing Workflow

```
A 4K photorealistic 3D render of the [YOUR LOGO] symbol, crafted from clean [COLOR] plastic material. 100px extrusion giving significant depth. Rotated +30 degrees on Y-axis. Pure non-reflective vault black background. White ambient light with physically correct shading (Octane render).
```

**Variables:** `[YOUR LOGO]`, `[COLOR]`
**Best for:** Retexturing existing logos with new materials
**Style:** 4K photorealistic, Octane render, vault black background
**Note:** Works best with uploaded reference image of existing logo

---

### 3.4 Branded Grillz Design

```
Luxurious high-end 3D product render of custom dental grills based on chrome wireframe aesthetic. Highly polished mirror-finish [MATERIAL] metal. Skeletal futuristic design with wireframe tooth outlines (not solid caps). [YOUR BRAND] logo/symbol prominently integrated into center of both upper and lower pieces in same polished chrome. Floating in minimalist studio against clean neutral light [COLOR] background. Precise studio lighting emphasizing metallic reflections.
```

**Variables:** `[MATERIAL]`, `[YOUR BRAND]`, `[COLOR]`
**Best for:** Luxury brand grillz concepts, chrome wireframe jewelry
**Style:** Mirror-finish chrome, wireframe skeletal design, studio lighting

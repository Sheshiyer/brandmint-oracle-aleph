# AI Logo Generation Prompts

## Concept 1: The Minimalist Symbol
**Idea:** Simple, geometric representation of [Core Concept].
**Midjourney Prompt:**
`/imagine prompt: flat vector logo of [Subject], minimalist, geometric, simple lines, clean, white background, Paul Rand style --no shading, realistic details, text`

## Concept 2: The Abstract Mark
**Idea:** Representing [Value, e.g., Speed] through shape.
**Midjourney Prompt:**
`/imagine prompt: abstract vector logo representing [Concept], dynamic shapes, modern, tech aesthetic, gradient [Color 1] to [Color 2], white background --no text, realistic`

## Concept 3: The Vintage Badge
**Idea:** Heritage and trustworthiness.
**Midjourney Prompt:**
`/imagine prompt: vintage badge logo for [Brand Name], [Industry], retro typography, seal style, thick lines, monochrome, white background --no color, photo`

## Concept 4: The Mascot
**Idea:** Character-based approach.
**Midjourney Prompt:**
`/imagine prompt: mascot logo of a [Animal/Character], friendly, vector illustration, esports style, bold outlines, flat colors, white background --no realism`

## Concept 5: The Typographic Monogram
**Idea:** Focus on the initials.
**Midjourney Prompt:**
`/imagine prompt: logo design of letter [Initial], creative typography, negative space, bold, modern, sans-serif, black on white background --no other text`

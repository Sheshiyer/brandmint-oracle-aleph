## Prompt

A Nano Banana prompt for visionaries. Works great for 16:9. 

Prompt:
Do this for Asimov:   1. The Analysis:  
      Analyze Input:   Identify the Visionary's Era (e.g., 1950s) and their Core Predictions (e.g., Robotics, AI).
      Left Side (The Origin):   Construct the room based on the  actual  historical workspace of the person (e.g., Typewriters, cigarette smoke, blueprints, tungsten light).
      Right Side (The Legacy):   Construct the room based on the  modern realization  of their ideas (e.g., Clean server room, holograms, advanced prosthetics, cool LED light).

  2. Composition (The Rift):  
      The Divide:   A diagonal beam of light splits the room.
      The Subject:   The Visionary stands dead center.
         Left Half of Body:  Dressed in period clothing, holding a pen/paper.
         Right Half of Body:  Fading into digital pixels or observing a floating hologram of their invention.
      The Wall:  
         Left Wall:  Covered in pinned sketches and paper notes.
         Right Wall:  A floor-to-ceiling window looking out onto a futuristic city (The world they predicted).

  3. Visual 
      Cohesion:   The perspective lines of the room must match perfectly across the split. A wooden desk on the left might morph into a sleek glass interface on the right.
      Lighting:   Warm/Dusty (Left) vs. Cold/Sterile (Right).

  Output:   ONE image, 16:9 Aspect Ratio, Cinematic Concept Art, Photorealistic.

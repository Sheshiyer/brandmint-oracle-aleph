## Prompt

Prompt:

A bold, vibrant, retro-American vector lettering design displaying the name "Figma".

TYPOGRAPHY STYLE (FAT SCRIPT):

The text is rendered in a thick, chunky, hand-lettered "baseball script" or "bubble" style, reminiscent of 1950s-1970s American sports logos and automotive decals.

Form: The letters are heavy, rounded, and tightly connected (ligatures).

Perspective: The text line should be slightly slanted upwards or arched to create dynamic energy.

Effects: Apply a thick, bold outline around the text. Crucially, add a deep, solid "block shadow" (extrusion) behind the letters to create a heavy 3D pop-out effect, characteristic of vintage sticker art.

COLOR PALETTE (BRAND VINTAGE):

Use a bright, saturated, and flat color palette inspired by [BRAND NAME]'s identity but filtered through a retro pop-art lens.

Fill: Solid, punchy colors (no gradients).

Contrast: High contrast between the letter fill, the outline (usually white or cream), and the block shadow (usually a darker shade or contrasting color).

DECORATIVE ELEMENTS:

Keep it clean and vector-like. You may include subtle, classic "Americana" design elements integrated into the text flow, such as a "swoosh" underline, a simple star, or a very simplified icon relevant to the brand (e.g., a lightning bolt for energy, a leaf for eco). Do NOT over-detail; keep it looking like a printed logo.

BACKGROUND:

Isolated on a clean, pure white background. No paper textures, no noise, no dirt.

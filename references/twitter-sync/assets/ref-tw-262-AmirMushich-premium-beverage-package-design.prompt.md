## Prompt

Prompt:

[BRAND NAME]. 
Act as a world-class product photographer creating an ultra-high-end editorial shot of a luxury beverage bottle.

COMPOSITION (FULL VIEW):

A full-body studio photograph showing the entire bottle from base to cap, centered in the frame. The bottle stands on a seamless surface.

THE VESSEL & COLORS (MATERIALITY):

Bottle Body: Premium, opaque matte white ceramic with a fine, powdery texture.

Bottle Cap: A premium matte or satin-finish cap colored exactly in [BRAND NAME]'s primary brand color.

LIGHTING & BACKGROUND (COLORED STUDIO):

The background is a seamless, smooth studio cyclorama flooded with [BRAND NAME]'s primary brand color, creating a rich, immersive color environment.

Lighting: Sophisticated, high-end white studio lighting (large softboxes). The light must be soft and wrapping, creating elegant highlights along the ceramic edges to define form.

CENTRAL BRANDING (ULTRA-PRECISION MICRO-EMBOSSING):

The main Gothic/Blackletter [BRAND NAME] text is centered on the bottle.

CRITICAL TEXTURE FIX: The text is rendered with ultra-subtle, shallow precision micro-embossing (maximum 0.2mm to 0.3mm depth). It must NOT look like applied clay or a heavy badge. It should look like a crisp, shallow tooling mark integrated perfectly into the ceramic surface, defined only by very subtle light catching the edges.

Color: The embossed text itself is colored in [BRAND NAME]'s primary brand color, perhaps with a slight semi-gloss finish to contrast subtly with the matte white bottle.

TYPOGRAPHIC HIERARCHY (AUXILIARY DETAILS):

All auxiliary text is printed cleanly in a neutral dark grey/black (monochrome) to sit quietly on the white surface:

Top Micro-Blocks: Two very small lines above main text (e.g., "LIMITED EDITION // BATCH 001").

Flavor Name: Below main text, a beautiful, smaller, unobtrusive font for the flavor.

Volume/Specs: Further down, technical info (e.g., "500ML").

Ingredients Block: At the very bottom edge, a dense micro-print block.

CORNER ELEMENTS:

Top Right: Small, discrete monochrome Gothic brand name.

Bottom Right: Small monochrome original brand logo.
